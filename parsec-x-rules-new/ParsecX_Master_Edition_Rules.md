# Parsec X: Master Edition Rulebook

Welcome to **Parsec X**, a micro-4X space simulation game for 2 to 4 players. This Master Edition rulebook integrates the base rules with the *Advanced Tactics*, *Flagships*, *Galactic Council*, and *Galactic Objectives* expansions, reflecting the latest V1.1 balance adjustments.

---

## 1. Components
- **Sector Map Cards:** 4 double-sided cards forming the 3x5 grid sectors.
- **Ship Cards & Tech Tree Cards:** 1 per player.
- **Civilization Cards:** 8 unique factions (Sharnak, The Conversation, Wulfram, Rim Worlds, Aiiji, Altair, Gaian, Purist).
- **Cubes:** 15 per player (11 for tracking/tech, 4 for ships).
- **Moon Cubes:** 8 Black (Shipyard), 8 White (Colony).
- **Objective Decks:** Stage 1, Stage 2, and Secret.
- **Agenda Deck:** 45 cards for the Galactic Council.
- **Flagship Extension Board:** 1 per player.
- **Tactic Deck:** 30 cards for advanced maneuvers.
- **Dice:** Ten 6-sided dice (10d6).

## 2. Setup
1. **Map:** Arrange 4 Sector Map cards based on player count. Each sector is a **3x5 grid**.
2. **Civilization:** Each player receives 2 Civilization cards, chooses one, and discards the other.
3. **Starting Fleet:** Each player places 2 ships (cubes) on their Homeworld sector.
4. **Resources:** Place tracking cubes on the starting resource markers on your Ship Card.
5. **[GalObj]** **Objectives:**
   - Deal 3 Stage 1 Public and 2 Stage 2 Public objectives face down. Reveal the first Stage 1 card.
   - Deal 2 Secret Objectives to each player; they keep one.
6. **[Flag]** **Flagship:** Every player starts with the right to designate one of their ships as a Flagship during the game.

---

## 3. The Galactic Round (Phase Structure)
A round consists of six sequential phases. Phases 1 and 2 are simultaneous; 3, 4, and 5 proceed in clockwise order starting with the Start Player.

### Phase 1: Upkeep
- **Resource Gathering:** Collect native yields from your Homeworld and any controlled planets.
- **[ATac]** **Tactic Cards:** Draw 1 Tactic Card from the deck.
- **[Flag]** **Jettison Flagship Extensions:** You may jettison one Flagship Extension to remove a Damage Token from your Flagship.

### Phase 2: Upgrade
- **Ship Construction:** Pay the indicated Ore/Credit cost to build new ships.
- **Tech Research:** Pay resources to move cubes down your Tech Tree (Engines, Command, Shields, Weapons).
- **[Flag]** **Flagship Designation:** If you have no Flagship, designate an existing ship as one.
- **[Flag]** **Extensions:** Buy unique modules for your Flagship (Cost: 2/4/6/8 resources for slots 1/2/3/4).

### Phase 4: Encounter
- **Exploration:** If entering a sector with an Unknown Planet, roll 1d6. On a 4+, you discover a planet and a moon.
- **Combat:** If you occupy a sector with an opponent's ship or planet, combat occurs.
  - **Strength:** Roll 1d6 + Weapons/Shields tech + Support (+1 for every additional friendly ship in the sector).
  - **[Flag]** **Flagships:** Count as 2 ships for support and have native strength bonuses.
  - **Ties:** If totals are equal, **both ships are destroyed** (**[Flag]** unless protected by a *Command Carrier Bay*).
  - **Retreat:** Before rolls, a defender may discard 2 Energy to move 1 space away.

### **[GalCon]** **Phase 5: Galactic Council
*Triggered once an Elder Civilization is claimed or all planets are revealed.*
1. **Convene:** The Start Player draws 1 Agenda card.
2. **Voting:** Players vote using **Influence** (1:1) and **Passive Voting Power** from planets.
   - *Homeworld:* +1 Vote (or +3 if Head of Diplomacy).
   - *Planetary Capital:* +3 Votes.
   - *Elder/Xenophobic:* +3 / +2 Votes.
3. **Resolution:** Highest total passes immediately.

### **Phase 6: Scoring
- **[GalObj]** **Public Objectives:** If you meet the criteria, place a cube on a Public Objective.
- **[GalObj]** **Secret Objectives:** Reveal and score one Secret Objective.
- **Victory:** The first player to reach the VP target (**Standard: 5, Long: 7, Epic: 10**) wins immediately.

## **[Flag]** **5. Flagship Appendix
- **Damage:** Flagships take 2 hits to destroy. The first hit grants a "Damage Token."
- **Extensions:** Unique modules built during Phase 2.
- **Death:** If destroyed, all Extensions are lost. You must rebuild from slot 1 if you designate a new hull.

# APPENDICES: THE EXPANSION CATALOGS

## APPENDIX A: THE TACTIC DECK [ATac]
Assemble 30 Tactic cards. Players draw 1 per round during Upkeep.

### Combat Tricks
1.  **Emergency Thrusters:** Ignore a combat loss; retreat to an adjacent sector immediately.
2.  **Shield Overload:** +3 Defense Strength for one roll.
3.  **Experimental Ordnance:** If winning, destroy 2 enemy ships instead of 1.
4.  **Bypass Codes:** Ignore a planet's native Defense bonuses (e.g. Capitals).
5.  **Flanking Maneuver:** (Attacker only) Re-roll combat die once.
6.  **Sabotage:** Negate opponent's Weapon/Shield tech bonus for this engagement.
7.  **Point Defense Grid:** Negate an opponent's Orbital Bombardment.
8.  **Kamikaze Protocol:** After losing, both your ship and the winning enemy are destroyed.

### Strategic Actions
9.  **Industrial Espionage:** Steal 2 Resources from another player.
10. **Trade Network Hack:** Swap the position of two of your ships anywhere.
11. **Smuggler’s Route:** One ship may move diagonally this turn.
12. **Deep Cover Agent:** Swap a Secret Objective with a new draw.
13. **Rapid Deployment:** Build 1 ship for free.
14. **Ghost Fleet:** Pass through sectors occupied by enemy ships without stopping.
15. **Resource Transmutation:** Convert all Ore to Credits.

### Uncharted Space & Politics
16. **Astrogation Master:** Dictate result of an Unknown Planet discovery.
17. **Distress Beacon:** Pull any ship directly to your Flagship.
18. **Anomalous Field:** Lockdown a Sector Map card for the round.
19. **Diplomatic Backchannels:** Automatically alliance with Elder Civilizations.
20. **Aggressive Terraforming:** Terraform for half cost.
21. **Veto Power:** Cancel a passed Council Resolution.
22. **Bribery:** Subtract up to 3 votes from an opponent.
23. **Political Assassination:** Prevent a specific player from being elected.
24. **Filibuster Delay:** Draw a new Agenda card immediately.
25. **Blackmail:** Dictate an opponent's passive planetary votes.

## APPENDIX B: FLAGSHIP EXTENSION CATALOG [Flag]
Install up to 4 modules. Costs: 2, 4, 6, 8 resources total.

### Combat Operations
1.  **Orbital Bombardment Array:** Roll 1d6. On 5+, destroy 1 defending ship before combat rolls.
2.  **Command Carrier Bay:** Protects other friendly ships in sector from tiebreaker losses.
3.  **Ablative Hull Plating:** +1 Passive Defense Strength.
4.  **Precision Targeting AI:** +1 Passive Attack Strength.
5.  **EMP Pulse Caster:** Spend 1 Energy to negate enemy tech bonuses.

### Navigation & Exploration
6.  **Quantum Slipstream Drive:** Spend 3 Energy to jump to any Spacewarp on the board.
7.  **Deep Space Sensors:** Roll 2 dice for Exploration; pick the best result.
8.  **Tractor Beam:** Forcefully drag one enemy ship from your starting sector to your destination.

### Logistics & Economy
9.  **Diplomatic Envoy Protocol:** Elder Civilization alliance cost reduced by 2.
10. **Scavenger Harvesters:** Gain 1 Ore/Credit after every combat victory.
11. **Advanced Logistics Hub:** Spawn new ships directly onto your Flagship's sector.
12. **Terraforming Charges:** Terraform cost reduced by 2 in current sector.

### Intergalactic Politics & Objectives
13. **Galactic Broadcast Node:** +3 Voting Power during Council.
14. **Stealth Recon Uplink:** Hold +1 additional Secret Objective.
15. **Mobile Command Center:** Flagship's sector counts as a controlled planet for scoring requirements.

## APPENDIX C: THE AGENDA DECK [GalCon]
40 Unique Cards split into Resolutions and Elections.

**Resolutions (For/Against):** Wormhole Tolls (Credit cost for warp), Demilitarized Zones (Influence required to attack), Imperial Taxation (Loss of Credits or ship), accelerated Deployment (Spawn on any controlled planet), etc.

**Elections (Elect Player/Planet):** Galactic Treasurer (+3 Credit Upkeep), Head of Diplomacy (+2 Inf, Homeworld = 3 votes), Public Enemy (VP gain for killers), Planetary Capital (This planet type = 3 votes), Cultural Preservation (Cannot be attacked).

---

## APPENDIX D: THE OBJECTIVE CARDS [GalObj]
45 unique cards divided into Stage 1, Stage 2, and Secret.

**Stage 1 (1 VP):** Expand Borders (Control 1 planet), Mighty Fleet (4 ships), Secure Sectors (Ships in 3 sectors), Mining Boom, Agrarian Push, Gas Harvesting, Political Influence (Spend 3 Influence), Flagship Commissioning (Built 1 Extension).

**Stage 2 (2 VP):** Galactic Dominance (2 planets outside Homeworld), Mastery of Science (Final tier tech), Centralize Command (Shipyard + Colony), Resource Monopoly (Farm + Mining), Terraformer, Juggernaut Pattern (4 Extensions built).

**Secret (1 VP):** Surprise Attack (Action Phase: Win combat at Unknown/Warp), Cultural Hub (2 planets of same type), Aggressive Expansion (In opponent's sector), Hoarder (8 of one resource), Capital Ship Kill (Action Phase: Destroy a Flagship).
