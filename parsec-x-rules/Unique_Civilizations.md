## Resources
There 4 types of resources in the game:
1. Ore (Orange)
2. Energy (Blue)
3. Credits (Yellow)
4. Influence (Purple)

## Civilizations
There are 8 unique civilizations in the game:

### Sharnak Imperium
* Convert 1 Credit to 1 Ore
* Upgrade: Weapons: -1 To Ore
* Encounter: Attack: 1 reroll of any one die during encounter (attack) phase. Must accept the second result.

### The Conversation
* Convert 1 Influence to 1 Credit
* Upgrade: Engines: -1 To Ore
* Upgrade: Command: -1 To Credits   

### Wulfram Collective
* Convert 1 Ore to 1 Influence
* Upkeep: Gain 1 Ore
* Encounter: Attack: 1 reroll of any one die during encounter (attack) phase. Must accept the second result.

### Rim Worlds Combine
* Convert 1 Ore to 1 Credit
* Upkeep: Gain 1 Credit
* Encounter: 1 reroll of any one die during encounter (Encountering an unknown planet) phase. Must accept the second result.

### The Aiiji
* Convert 1 Influence to 1 Credit
* Upkeep: Gain 1d3 Credits
* Encounter: Command: 1 reroll of any one die during encounter phase. Must accept the second result.

### Altair Divide
* Convert 1 Ore to 1 Influence, 1 Influence to 1 Credit
* Upgrade: Weapons: -1 To Energy
* Encounter: Attack: Prevent reroll.

### Gaian Empire
* Convert 1 Credit to 1 Influence
* Upgrade: Shield: -1 To Energy
* Encounter: Defense: 1 reroll of any one die during encounter (Defense) phase. Must accept the second result.

### Purist Hegemony
* Convert 1 Influence to 1 Ore
* Upgrade: Command: -1 To Energy
* Encounter: Defense: +1 to die roll.
