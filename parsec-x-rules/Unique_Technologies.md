# Ship technology trees

Ships are coming in two flavors: "Wanderer-A" and "Raider-B". Before game you select a flavor for your ships type. You cannot mix them.

There are 4 technology trees:

## Weapons

### Wanderer-A

Levels: 
1. Rail gun (Default)
    * Effect: +1 Attack
2. Gauss Missle
    * Effect: +3 Attack, 
    * Price: 1 Credits, 1 Energy, 3 Ore
3. Fusion Cannot
    * Effect: +5 Attack, 
    * Price: 2 Credits, 2 Energy, 2 Ore
4. Antimatter Cannot
    * Effect: +6 Attack, 
    * Price: 4 Credits, 3 Energy, 1 Ore

### Raider-B

Levels: 
1. Rail gun (Default)
    * Effect: +2 Attack
2. Gauss Missle
    * Effect: +4 Attack, 
    * Price: 3 Credits, 1 Energy, 3 Ore
3. Fusion Cannot
    * Effect: +6 Attack, 
    * Price: 3 Credits, 2 Energy, 3 Ore
4. Antimatter Cannot
    * Effect: +7 Attack, 
    * Price: 3 Credits, 3 Energy, 3 Ore

## Command

### Wanderer-A

Levels:
1. Navicomm  (Default)
    * No benefits
2. Veteran Captain
    * Action cost: 1 influence, 
    * Effect: +1 to die roll during the Encounter Phase, 
    * Price: 2 Credits, 2 Energy, 1 Influence
3. Drone AI
    * Action cost: 1 energy,
    * Effect: Reroll a die during the Encounter Phase. The player must accept the second result.
    * Price: 3 Credits, 2 Energy, 2 Influence
4. Shipmind
    * Action cost: 1 energy,
    * Effect: Reroll a die twice during the Encounter Phase. The player may accept any value rolled.
    * Price: 4 Credits, 2 Energy, 2 Influence

### Raider-B
