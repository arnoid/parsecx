# Expansion: Galactic Objectives

This expansion overhauls the victory conditions of Parsec X, introducing a dynamic layer of strategic goals that players must complete to earn Victory Points (VP).

## 🧩 The Objective Deck
A deck of **45 Objective Cards** is used in every game. These cards are categorized into four tactical domains:

*   **Military (MIL):** (e.g., "The Admiral" - Have a fleet of 4 ships; "War Profiteer" - Win 3 combats).
*   **Technology (TECH):** (e.g., "Polymath" - Have all technologies at Level 2).
*   **Economy (ECON):** (e.g., "Titan of Industry" - Have 10 Credits; "Energy Grid" - Have 8 Energy).
*   **Expansion (EXP):** (e.g., "Colonizer" - Control 3 planets).
*   **Diplomacy (DIP):** (e.g., "High Councilor" - Cast 15 total votes in the Council).

---

## 🏗️ New Setup Rules
1.  **Public Objectives:** At the start of the game, shuffle the Objective Deck and reveal **3 Public Objective cards**.
2.  **Shared Goals:** These objectives are available to **all players**. Multiple players can complete and claim the same objective.

---

## 🤫 Secret Objectives
In addition to the Public Objectives, every player has a private path to victory:

1.  **Private Goals:** During setup, each player is dealt **1 Secret Objective** card from the deck.
2.  **Hidden Knowledge:** You do not reveal this card to other players until you have completed the objective.
3.  **VP Value:** Just like Public Objectives, a Secret Objective is worth **1 VP**.
4.  **No Double Dipping:** You cannot claim the same objective twice (e.g., if it appears both as a Public and your Secret card, you only gain 1 VP).

---

## 🏆 Scoring & Victory
The win condition is no longer purely based on planet count. Points are awarded at the end of each round (Phase 4):

*   **Planetary Control:** Each planet you control is worth **1 VP**.
*   **Objective Claim:** Each Public Objective you complete is worth **1 VP**.
    *   Once you claim an objective, it is yours for the rest of the game.
    *   Objectives are checked and awarded automatically at the end of the Encounter Phase.

**Victory Target:** The first player to reach the required **VP Target** (default 5, 7, or 10 VP) wins the game.
